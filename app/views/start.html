Thank you for testing the new SIEL application form designs.

The following pages are prototypes, so there are some caveats and assumptions:

  • some links and buttons will work, others will not
  • the task sections are static, but in the real form, you'll only see sections that are relevant to your application
  • all sections are open for editing, but in the real form you'll be asked to fill them out in order
  • the prototypes do not save your data, so you won't see information you've entered on summary pages, and 'incomplete' labels won't change to 'complete'
  • some 'auto-complete' questions need to be manually typed
  • all questions are optional in the prototypes, but many will be mandatory in the real form
