Help with naming your product

The product name should be specific enough to help case officers know exactly what the item is.

Where appropriate it should include the make, model and type of item. It should not contain serial numbers or part numbers.

A descriptive product name will also help you identify and use it in any future applications.

If you are applying for a kit containing multiple items, you'll need to upload a product description document that specifies and describes all items in the kit.
