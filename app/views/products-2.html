Help with components, accessories and technology

A component is a constituent part or element of the product.

An accessory is an optional item that when fitted to a functioning product adds features or capability. An accessory can also be removed without detriment to the product.

'Technology' means information necessary for the development, production or use of goods or software. For example, specifications, engineering drawings and user manuals.
