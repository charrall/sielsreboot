Help with end user, consignee and third party

An end-user receives the products in the destination country. They either use the products themselves, resell from stock, or export them again to another country.

A consignee receives the products and then delivers them to the end-user.

A third party is involved in the export but not regarded as a consignee, end-user or ultimate end-user, for example they might be an agent, broker, consultant or distributor.

For products being processed or incorporated and then exported again passed their first destination, there may also be an ultimate end-user. An ultimate end-user in a third country receives goods via an onward export from the end user.
